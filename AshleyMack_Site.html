About Ashley

Ashley Mack is a corporate transformation leader and Board Chair of the National Association of Asian American Professionals, the nation's oldest and largest Asian professional organization. As the youngest Executive Director in its history, she led national strategy across 30 chapters and 27,000 stakeholders, delivering 143% revenue growth and strengthening enterprise partnerships nationwide.

With over 15 years of experience across newly launched organizations and Fortune 100 companies, Ashley leads enterprise-scale transformation initiatives and oversees multi-million-dollar portfolios. She directs cross-functional global teams and partners with C-suite leaders to align strategy with execution and deliver measurable results.

Known for building influence quickly in high-stakes environments, Ashley helps professionals translate ambiguity into structured action and scalable performance.

A 2025 Outstanding Atlanta Honoree and 2023 Atlanta Business Chronicle 40 Under 40 Honoree, Ashley speaks on power, influence, executive presence, and strategic visibility in complex organizations.
